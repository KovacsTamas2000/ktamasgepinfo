Northern Part of Baldur's Gate

Ducal Palace is the large building in the center of the map. It is where the debate over the cause of the iron crisis and the possibility of war with Amn takes place. It is inaccessible until the final chapter of Baldur's Gate.

Bheren's Home: Is the west-most house in a row of three houses by the palace gates. This building is associated with the side quest Petrine's Cat.

Rinnie's Home: Is the middle house in a row of three houses by the palace gates. This building is associated with the side quest A Bard's Request.

Luisa's Home: Is the east-most house in a row of three houses by the palace gates. This building is not associated with any quests.

Jardak's Home: Located right next to the Temple of Helm.
